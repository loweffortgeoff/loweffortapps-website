Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

p>

This Privacy Policy explains what information our apps collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. Our apps are designed to run locally on your device, without accounts, analytics, or unnecessary data collection.

Our Applications

Low Effort Apps ("we", "us", or "our") designs, releases, and operates the following mobile applications:

  • AisleWise
  • Task(ed)
  • Yes? No? Go!
  • Muzzletoff
  • Elapse(D)
  • Routine(e)
  • Loud Sky
  • Trackked
  • Wottle Bosh
  • Offshoots

Information We Collect

Apps That Collect No Data:

Wottle Bosh, Trackked, AisleWise, Task(ed), Yes? No? Go!, Routine(e), Loud Sky, Offshoots and Muzzletoff do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted off-device.

Elapse(D) uses Location Data:

The Elapse(D) app may request access to your approximate or precise location. This is used solely to display sunrise, sunset, or other time-based information relevant to your location.

  • Location data is processed on-device only
  • It is not stored, logged, or shared
  • You may deny or revoke access anytime in device settings
  • The app continues to function, but certain features may be limited
  • We do not use this information for tracking or advertising

How Your Information Is Used

  • Apps listed above that collect no personal data use no data for any purpose.
  • Elapse(D) uses location solely to determine and display time-relevant information.

Data Storage & Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. The data remains until modified or until you delete the app. No off-device copies exist.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our apps are safe for use without parental consent under COPPA and similar laws.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or disclose personal information to third parties for advertising, analytics, or tracking purposes.

Elapse(D)'s optional AI chatbot sends user-entered text to Google's Gemini API solely to generate responses. This data is not stored by Low Effort Apps, is not associated with users, and is processed according to Google's API terms and privacy policies.

Our apps use no analytics services, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or cloud storage.

Your Choices

  • Revoke Elapse(D) location permissions anytime in your device's Settings.
  • Delete app data anytime by deleting the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes take effect once posted here.
